Services of Nirmani studio

We are here to serve you our level best...

Services of Nirmani studio can be clarified into 4 main categories which are photo editing, Wedding photography, event photography and other requirements. For all categories we have best professional photographers with us in order to give you the best what we have.
We are always ready to give you the maximum service with all our aspects, as Nirmani Studio can be considered as one of the leading and popular studio in the industry. As we all care is about the clients' satisfaction, we ensure that we provide the best service.

Photo editing and framing

Within photo editing service, we recover old and damaged photographs and as well as edit given photograph by adding some creativity and fantasy beyond the limits and boundaries. We have one of the best photos framing crew to add more value to your photographs.

Wedding photography

Wedding photography is the most popular service category in the studio. With a dedicated team of photographers, cinematographers, video editors and photo editors we create most promising story telling albums. Event photography

Other requirements
